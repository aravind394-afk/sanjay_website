About Hasthas

Scientific equipment made to support dependable laboratory work.

Established in 2011, Hasthas Scientific Instruments India P. Ltd. is engaged in manufacturing, supplying, trading and wholesaling scientific instruments and equipment. The company’s range includes scientific instruments, temperature-bath instruments and water-bath instruments.

The equipment is intended for applications in biotechnology, meteorology, educational institutions, hospitals, research and development facilities and industrial laboratories.

  • Cost-effective product options
  • Compact, low-maintenance designs
  • Support for bulk requirements
  • Multiple designs and specifications
